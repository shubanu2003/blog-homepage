Our Story

The story of trips begins with a simple yet profound love for travel. It started with a single trip to kerala, where we discovered the joy of exploring new cultures, savoring unique cuisines, and soaking in breathtaking landscapes. What began as a personal journey soon evolved into a shared passion for discovering the world.

Kerala is known for its beautiful backwaters and lush landscapes, which offers a unique and tranquil experience.
